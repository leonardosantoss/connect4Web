Game Instructions

First, you need to choose the board size, as well as who you play against. You can choose to play against another player locally or against a bot. If the bot is chosen, you need to select the difficulty (easy, medium, hard or hardcore). You can also select the color you play with, as well as the bot/other player color.

To place a checker you need to click on the desired column, and then the checker will be fall to the the lowest empty position on given column. If the column is full, the checker will not be placed. You should then choose another column.

To win Connect Four you must be the first player to get four of your colored checkers in a row either horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
